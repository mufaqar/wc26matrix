SCENE 02 Who is Curaçao — The island nation explained
Voiceover
Let's start with the basics. Curaçao is a small island in the southern Caribbean Sea, located about 40 miles north of Venezuela. It's a constituent country within the Kingdom of the Netherlands — yes, it's technically part of the Netherlands, but it runs its own football association. The capital is Willemstad, famous for its colorful Dutch colonial architecture. The population is roughly 150,000 people. To put that in perspective, more people live in the stands of Houston Stadium during a World Cup match than live in the entire country. Curaçao is the smallest nation by both population and land size to ever qualify for a FIFA World Cup.
