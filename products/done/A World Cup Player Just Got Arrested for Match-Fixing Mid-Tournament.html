SCENE 03 What is spot-fixing — Fixing specific moments (yellow cards, corners) vs fixing the whole match
Voiceover
Spot-fixing is different from match-fixing. You don't fix the result. You fix a specific moment — a yellow card at a certain minute, a corner kick, a throw-in. These moments are bets on micro-events. They're harder to detect, easier to payout, and they're exploding in modern football because of the depth of betting markets.
