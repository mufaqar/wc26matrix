SCENE 04 What is the FIFA Pass visa system?
Voiceover
FIFA has a system for this — the FIFA Pass. It's supposed to be a fast-track visa arrangement that guarantees entry for players, officials, and staff traveling for FIFA-sanctioned matches. The host country agrees to honor these passes as part of the tournament hosting agreement. In theory, a player with a FIFA Pass should breeze through immigration. In practice? The FIFA Pass is not a legally binding document. It's a request. The host country's immigration laws always supersede. And when politics gets involved, the FIFA Pass becomes just a piece of paper.

SCENE 05 How World Cup player access normally works

It works for most countries. But for nations on the US travel restriction list, the Host Agreement doesn't apply. Iraq is one of those nations. And that's where the system breaks.
